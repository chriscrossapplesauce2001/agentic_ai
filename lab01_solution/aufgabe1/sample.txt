Common Area Moment of Inertia Formulas
========================================

Rectangle (about centroidal x-axis):
  Ix = (b * h^3) / 12
  where b = width, h = height

Circle (about centroidal axis):
  Ix = (pi * d^4) / 64
  where d = diameter

Hollow circle / tube:
  Ix = (pi / 64) * (D^4 - d^4)
  where D = outer diameter, d = inner diameter

I-beam (use parallel axis theorem):
  Divide into rectangles, compute each Ix about its own centroid,
  then shift using: I_total = sum(Ix_i + A_i * d_i^2)
  where A_i = area of part i, d_i = distance from part centroid to overall centroid

Example: Steel beam with rectangular cross-section 200mm x 400mm
  b = 0.2 m, h = 0.4 m
  Ix = (0.2 * 0.4^3) / 12 = 0.001067 m^4
